The new social movement

As the youth, we have been disproportionately affected by the attention-based business models of the largest tech companies. We've had enough.

The problem

• Social media companies make money by selling our time, attention, and information:
working to addict us to their services and maximize engagement on every post.

• This business model has wrought mental illness, loneliness, and political polarization on a global scale, with no signs of slowing down.

• We aim to influence the system through organized boycotts, public pressure, and signal-boosting safer alternatives.

Power in numbers

We are claiming back our agency by working together. Is there a specific website or app that is taking away your time? Here is a community of people with the exact same goals.

Blue skies

As much as it has so far been used against us, technology can be used for good. By bringing these issues into the public eye on campus and beyond, we're working towards a future where technlogy works for EVERYONE's best interests.
